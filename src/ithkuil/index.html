Dev Notes

0.1 (28 jul '14) : If you read this, you're one of the first users of Ithkuil Help (thanks !). Please report all your remarks/notes/critics on the facebook page ! You can contact me on facebook too ("Bastien E. A. Michaux").

Why isn't it working ?

Be online in order for the page to work. On stypi, the page may run slowly. Don't wanna wait ? Copy-paste the code ("ctrl + a") in your notepad and save the file as "something.html". Then open the page with your internet browser, Ithkuil Help will work perfectly !

Is the work done ?

Not yet sadly -_- Basic morphology works, most cases too, incorporation is incorporated, the verbal morphology still needs some tweaks, and suffixes and adjuncts are for later. You probably noticed there are only a few roots, that's because I'm focusing on the grammar (I need approximately 20 minutes to encode a complete root + it's translation). Ithkuil is complicated, plus english isn't my mother tongue, I need thus time to decode/code/encode stuff. Here is the list of what Ithkuil Help will/should/could focus on :

  • Words 100% accurate !
  • Personal reference adjuncts !
  • More roots and suffixes ! (I need YOU !)
  • More word-building possibilities !
  • Figure out how to incorporate stress !
  • Better webdesign !
  • Dynamic grammar tips !
  • Rough english translation !
  • Comparison stuff !
  • Numbers !
  • Phonotactic rules !
  • Syntax stuff !
  • Responsive-design tweaks ! (so you can ithkuil on your mobile !)
  • All roots and suffixes ! (won't be done before 2015 IMHO)
  • Accurate english translation ! (won't be done before 2215 IMHO)

Christian Oudard also works on a more ambitious Ithkuil project (with the native script !), but I limit myself to simpler things for the time being. Check it on github !

Can you help ?

If you know html/css/javascript/jquery, obviously yes ! If you can use Excel or Word, you can help me greatly too, please contact me via facebook or bastienmichaux@gmail.com. I also need good english speakers to work on an automated and roughly accurate translation. If you want to translate Ithkuil in international french, I'm interested too !

How much time have I spent on this ?

Approximately 26 hours as of july 28. I began writing this on july 10 and spent a LOT of time encoding the affix tables. It was SO BORING (╬ ಠ益ಠ) that's why I don't want to work on bigger tables (translation, roots, suffixes) all alone. Also I play music and make other websites and love doing that. So, if you want a complete Ithkuil tool before the End-Times, let's share the work.
